My Summer Vacation

Traveling

This summer my family and I traveled to Bali--a small island in Indonesia. It was definetly the highlight of my summer and my favorite trip I've been on yet. In Bali we went rafting, snorkeling, and explored the unique culture on the island. Traveling somewhere new is always an adventure becasue because you never know what you might find.

Running

Running and I have a love/hate relationship. On one hand, I love running because I get to see the city in a different way. When you are running everything else in life kind of disapates and it is just you and the open road. On the other hand, running is hard especially when you live in a desert. If you want to run in the summer you have to get up very early, or you have to go really late at night. This is always a dilema because I like running in the morning, but I also like my beauty sleep. Another thing about running is its vey hard... running for an extended period of time takes a lot of hard work and dedication.

Exploring

At the end of the summer I drove up to Flagstaff with two of my friends on a 'mini vacation' to escape the heat. My sister has a small apartmnet there because she is attending NAU this year. It was a nice little trip where we go to explore the town and run in the cool, brisk air.
